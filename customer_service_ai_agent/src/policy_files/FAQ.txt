Frequently Asked Questions

ORDER QUESTIONS:

Q: How can I track my order?
A: Use your order number at our tracking portal or call customer service.
   You'll receive tracking information via email once your order ships.

Q: Can I modify my order after placing it?
A: Orders can be modified within 2 hours of placement if not yet processed.
   Contact customer service immediately for assistance.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, PayPal,
   Apple Pay, and financing through Affirm for qualified customers.

Q: Do you offer student discounts?
A: Yes! Students receive 10% off with valid .edu email verification.

SHIPPING QUESTIONS:

Q: What are your shipping options?
A: Standard (5-7 business days), Express (2-3 business days),
   Overnight (next business day), and Same-Day (select cities).

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Currently shipping to US and Canada. International expansion planned for 2025.

Q: What if my package is damaged?
A: Contact us immediately with photos. We'll arrange replacement or refund.

TECHNICAL QUESTIONS:

Q: What warranty comes with products?
A: Laptops: 1-year manufacturer warranty + optional extended warranty
   Accessories: 90 days to 1 year depending on product
   Cables: 30 days standard warranty

Q: Can I upgrade my laptop after purchase?
A: Many laptops support RAM and storage upgrades. Check compatibility
   guide or contact technical support for specific models.

Q: What software comes pre-installed?
A: Basic productivity software and drivers. Full software list available
   on each product page.

ACCOUNT QUESTIONS:

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Use "Forgot Password" on login page or contact customer service.

Q: Can I have multiple shipping addresses?
A: Yes, you can save multiple addresses in your account profile.

Q: How do I delete my account?
A: Contact customer service. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

RETURN QUESTIONS:

Q: What's your return policy?
A: See our detailed Return Policy document for complete information.

Q: Who pays for return shipping?
A: We provide prepaid return labels for defective items.
   Customer pays return shipping for change of mind returns.
